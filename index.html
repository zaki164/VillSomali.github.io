About The Government

Federal Government Of Somalia (FGS)   is the internationally recognized government of Somalia, and the first attempt to create a central government in Somalia since the collapse of the Somali Democratic Republic. It replaced the Transitional Federal Government of Somalia on 20 August 2012 with the adoption of the Constitution"
"of Somalia"

Mohamed Abdullahi “Farmajo" is a Somali politician, the 9th and current President of Somalia. He became the President of Somalia after winning in the 2017 Somali presidential election with 194 votes out of a total of 328 by members of the Somali parliament
